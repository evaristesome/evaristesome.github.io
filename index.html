Evariste-Some-LinkedIn 		Evariste Somé received Ph.D in Telecommunication Engineering, Master in Technology Cybersecurity & Policy degrees from the University of Colorado Boulder in 2023; Master and Bachelor degrees in Electrical Engineering from Temple University - PA. He is a Temporary Researcher in the Computer Science Department, University of Colorado. Somé's research interests are in wireless communication networks, with emphasis on antenna array and applications. Current research directions include Machine Learning (ML) and specifically Deep Learning for wireless communication systems, Integration of Sensing and Communication. Somé has been an active member of IEEE, SpectrumX , National Radio Dynamic Zone discussion groups, and IEEE Vehicular Technology Society (VTS) Committee on AI in Wireless Communications.
