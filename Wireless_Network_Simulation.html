Simulator Layers

  • Network Layer: Has knowledge about multiple nodes in the network. Functions include routing, forwarding addressing, sequencing, and congestion control.
  • Data Link Layer: Facilitates communication between directly connected nodes in a network. It has 2-sublayer. LLC: is concerned with flow control and error checking. MAC is concerned with physical addressing and channel access. A channel access refers to allowing multiple physical devices to access the same physical communication medium
  • PHY Layer: Is to convey or receive digital information over the medium.
